PTA Membership and Will Rogers Fund

Take the time now to JOIN WILL ROGERS PTA for only $11. Through PTA membership, you stay connected to the parent community and add your voice to the decision-making process for student advocacy at WILL ROGERS. Our 2025/26 Membership goal is to get 100% participation.
