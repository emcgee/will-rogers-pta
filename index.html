Welcome!

The Will Rogers Parent Teacher Association (PTA) is made up of parents, family members, teachers, staff and community members dedicated to positively impacting the lives of all children and families at Will Rogers Learning Community, an International Baccalaureate World Elementary School in Santa Monica, California.

PTA Reflections 2026-27

This year's theme: “What My Culture Means to Me”. Students are invited to create original works of art in dance, film, literature, music, photography and visual arts. Submissions are due October 16, 2026.

Join PTA

PTA is the largest children’s advocacy organization in the nation. Membership is $11, and demonstrates that education is important to you. Every member is eligible to vote on important decisions happening at our school during PTA association meetings each month.

+ Sign Up

Donate

Will Rogers PTA is a 501(c)3 organization, and all donations are tax-deductible. Making a direct donation makes the biggest impact, and supports every student and every classroom by helping us pay for field trips, library support, community events, classroom supplies, and much more.

PTA Meetings & Agendas

PTA meetings are usually held at 9:00 a.m. on the first Wednesday of each month, in-person in the library with a virtual component for those who need to join from home. Agendas are posted and can be reviewed prior to each PTA meeting.

Volunteer

Our PTA is a volunteer organization made up of parents, family members, teachers, staff and community members just like you who want to make a positive impact in the lives of students. There are many ways to get involved, from joining a planning committee to signing up to help at an event.
