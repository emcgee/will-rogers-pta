PTA Executive Board

PLEASE NOTE: Each PTA email address can be finished with “willrogerspta.com” to complete the email. Example: email@willrogerspta.com

Our executive board is made up of volunteers who are elected each year in March by the PTA association members. The board meets monthly to oversee the management of the PTA.

PTA Committee Chairs

Our Committee chairpeople are appointed by the PTA president, and oversee the work on individual committees which are formed to focus volunteer efforts in specific areas of the work of the PTA. Committee chairs call meetings throughout the year as needed, and report about the work of their committee at the monthly PTA association meetings.

Beautification

Robin Boden

beautification@

This committee meets 2-3 times a year to spruce up the campus! This includes weeding and planting around classrooms and play yards, trash pick-up, cleaning, and other projects identified by the committee chairperson. This committee also helps with watering plants throughout the school year. Beautification will work closely to coordinate with Family Farm Days!

Book Fair

Mia Bayuga

bookfair@

This committee works to plan two book fair events per school year, hosting classroom previews and on-campus sales throughout the event.

Cinco de Mayo Festival

OPEN Position

Works with the school to plan the annual Cinco de Mayo festival, provides volunteer support and other important work.

Family Dance

OPEN Position

Family Dance is a beloved tradition at Will Rogers. Held annually in the spring, the Family Dance has expanded to include a Fall Dance and both events are open to all students and families and include music, snacks, decorations, and more.

Grown Ups' Night Out & Auction

Evan McGee / Kate Gammon / Lisa Glasser

auction@

This committee works in the fall to plan a fun night-out event for parents & guardians, teachers & staff, which includes an exciting on-site auction, featuring items solicited by the fundraising subcommittee.

Jog-a-Thon

Kristin Marion, Zayna Abi-Hashim

jogathon@

This committee forms in the winter to plan for the spring jog-a-thon. This fundraiser is a fun event for all students, and promotes health & wellness, plus working together to meet a common goal while raising money for our school.

Kindergarten / TK Events

OPEN Position

This committee works to ensure that incoming TK & kindergarten families feel welcome and informed. Work includes a playdate in August, support for the school's fall “Meet and Greet” and spring “Kindergarten Round-up” events, plus back-to-school welcome activities like the Boo-Hoo breakfast and first day photos.

Membership Chair

Pending

membership@

Movie Nights

Caron Pelletier and Tom Morhous

movienights@

Works with VP & president to schedule and execute 2 or more movie nights per school year. Utilizes volunteers to plan, monitor, set-up and break down events.

Regenerative Farm Committee

Lila Daruty, Paola Vezzulli & Jodi Strock

This committee works to support the school's efforts to maintain the outdoor classroom space known as the Regenerative Farm. Administration and teachers direct programmatic plans and the committee works to provide volunteers to carry out those plans including on-site volunteers and grant writing.

Reflections Art Program

Kellie Kay

reflections@

The Reflections chairperson works with Santa Monica-Malibu PTA Council to establish timelines and learn guidance about running this national PTA program at our school. They work to promote details about the program to the community, collect submissions, and recruit judges. Winning submissions are then forwarded to SMMPTA to be judged against other winners in the Council. The chairperson celebrates all entries with a small event and provides certificates to participants.

Student Store

OPEN Position

studentstore@

Maintain and reorder student store merchandise. Recruit and manage volunteers and in-person sales in front of school and at school events.

5th Grade Events

Pending

The 5th grade committee works with teachers and administrators to plan fun and meaningful events for the 5th graders each year including a field trip, dance party, memory book, and supporting the school's culmination event.
