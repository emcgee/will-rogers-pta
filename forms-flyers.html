PTA Officer Nomination

Download

PTA elections take place in March. A nominating committee is formed in November (or early January) and accepts names to consider for nomination to the slate presented at least 28 days before the election.

Honorary Service Award Nomination

Each spring, PTA recognizes individuals whose volunteerism in service of students and community is above and beyond the norm. Nominations open in the spring, and awardees are recognized in May.
