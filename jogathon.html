Jog-a-Thon

Jog-a-thon is one of the biggest and most beloved events of the school year!

This fun and healthy event brings a sense of joy and community that we all know and love! Please make sure to join us for the school-wide assemblies, participate in the Jog-a-thon activities and ask your friends and family to donate to support our PTA.

Jog-a-Thon 2027 is coming up on March 19, 2027! Students will be able to build their own fundraising page to let friends and family know about their participation in this exciting and fun event.

Registration and volunteer sign-ups will be announced in the weekly newsletter closer to the event. Questions? Contact jogathon@willrogerspta.com.

Earn Prizes!

Class Incentives

  • Classes who have 100% of students sign up get a popsicle party!
  • Class with most money raised gets a pizza party!

Student Incentives

  • Register for a free personal fundraising page and be entered in our weekly raffle!
  • $20+ Squishee Toy!
  • $150+ Fun Swag Bag!
  • Every student who raises $300 or more will be invited to a special Ice Cream Social party!
  • Top 5 student fundraisers will take a field trip with Principal Daruty!

School Incentives

  • If we raise $45,000, we get a special school-wide surprise from Principal Daruty!

Thank you to our sponsors!

Jog-a-Thon 2027 Schedule

Jog-a-Thon festivities will happen for all students and all classes during the school day on March 19, 2027 on the Track/Field. The day's per-grade schedule and volunteer sign-ups will be announced closer to the event.
